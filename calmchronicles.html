About

Calm Chronicles is an online counselling platform run by Oladunjoye Rasheedah Olabisi. It is a form of professional mental health counselling that is generally performed through the internet using online mediums such as mobile apps, video conferencing, text messages, emails etc. Computer aided technologies are used by our set of trained professional counsellors and individuals seeking counselling services to communicate rather than the conventional face-to-face interaction. Established in 2023, C-C has risen greatly by our unexpected successes and the popular demand for our services.

Our services ranges from Virtual counselling sessions to chat/phone counselling, online support groups, self help resources, mindfulness and relaxation exercises, specialized counselling etc. These services aim to support users in managing their mental health and well- being through accessible and convenient online channels like us.
